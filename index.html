About us

confectioner & baker

Welcome to our website! Here you will find unique desserts created with love and skill by our head pastry chef, Alena. Alena has always been passionate about baking and spent a lot of time in the kitchen since childhood, helping her grandmother prepare the most delicious homemade cakes and cookies. This passion for cooking has stayed with her throughout her life and ultimately led to the creation of this website. After completing culinary courses and internships with renowned pastry chefs, Alena has become a pastry chef capable of creating true works of art from flour and sweets. Her desserts not only amaze with their appearance but also have a unique taste that is remembered for a long time. On our website, you can learn more about Alena's creativity, see her stunning cakes and desserts, as well as place an order for your event or enjoy delicious sweets at home. We are happy to see you here and share our love for the art of pastry-making with you!
